<p><h2>Family Law</h2></p>

<p>Family Law is a very complex area and no two cases are the same. At Patrick W. McGonagle &</br>
Co,our solicitors are available to provide you expert advice where a marriage or relationship has</br>
broken down irretrievably or is on the verge of a breakdown.</br></p>

<p>We advise our clients in a practical manner, set out clearly the options available to them, whilst</br>
considering the financial, taxation, succession and welfare issues involved. We aim to get our</br>
clients the best and fairest results possible through active negotiations and mediation between </br>
the parties. Where this does not prove possible we vigorously pursue Separation, Divorce and</br>
other related Family Law Proceedings through the Irish Courts.</br></p>

<p>Our specialised areas of expertise are:</br></p>

<p><li>Separation by Agreement</li>
<li>Judicial Separation</li>
<li>Divorce</li>
<li>Nullity</li>
<li>Property Adjustments/Transfer of Assets</li>
<li>Guardianship</li>
<li>Child Custody & Access</li>
<li>Cohabitation Agreements</li></p>
