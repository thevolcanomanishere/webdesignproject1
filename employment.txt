<p><h2>Employment Law</h2></p>

The area of Employment Law is constantly changing. Good Employment working relationships are a</br>
vital component of a modern successful business. We offer practical advise and support in this</br>
difficult and complex area. We represent clients both employers and employees at the Employment</br>
Appeals Tribunal, Labour Relations Commission and in the Courts.</br>

Our advices in this area include:-</br>

<li>Unfair Dismissal.</li>
<li>Redundancy</li>
<li>Bullying and Harassment at work</li>
<li>Work Related Stress Issues</li>
<li>Grievance Procedures</li>
<li>Creation and Review of Employment Contracts</li>
<li>Creation and Review of Employment Handbooks</li>
<li>Termination of Employment Contracts</li>
<li>Confidential Agreements</li>
<li>Reviewing Employment Policies and Procedures</li>
<li>Transfer of Undertakings</li>
